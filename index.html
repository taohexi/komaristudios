KOMARI

Mindful art, design, and storytelling

Komari is rooted in the Japanese craft of temari — hand‑stitched thread balls, formed from remnants of silk kimonos.

Wrapped with patience and care, each one held a quiet wish for a brilliant life.

We carry that meaning forward — creating with intention, honoring tradition, and shaping new narratives.
