Our Story

A small ball. A whole world.

The name Komari is rooted in the Japanese craft of temari, hand stitched thread balls first shaped over a thousand years ago from remnants of silk kimonos. Wrapped, divided, and embroidered with patient geometric care, each temari held the maker's quiet wish for a brilliant life.

We carry that meaning forward. Komari, a small ball, is a reminder of the world we share: a small sphere in a vast universe, wrapped in varied cultures, connected by common threads.

Komari Studios creates where art, design, and narrative converge with purpose. We create, curate, and collaborate with care, honoring the traditions and communities that shape our work.
